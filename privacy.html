Privacy Policy

ViewPhoto.TV is designed to work without accounts, without permanent storage, and without collecting personal information.

1. Overview

ViewPhoto.TV allows users to display photos from their phone on a TV or computer using a temporary QR‑code session. We intentionally avoid collecting or storing personal data.

2. What Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, emails, phone numbers, or passwords
  • No accounts or registration
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No tracking across websites
  • No permanent storage of photos

3. Temporary Photo Storage (Cloudflare)

To deliver photos between your phone and the screen, ViewPhoto.TV uses temporary storage on Cloudflare infrastructure (R2 / edge cache). This storage is strictly temporary and exists only to support the active session.

  • Photos exist only during the active session
  • Default session lifetime: 180 minutes
  • You can end the session at any time
  • When the session ends, all photos are deleted automatically
  • No photos are stored permanently
  • No photos are analyzed, indexed, or used for training
  • No photos are shared with third parties

Cloudflare acts only as a temporary transport layer. All communication is encrypted via HTTPS.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics. Local Storage may be used only for UI preferences (e.g., dark mode).

5. QR Code & Security

  • QR codes contain only a temporary session ID
  • Session IDs are random and expire automatically
  • All communication uses HTTPS
  • No personal data is embedded in the QR code

6. Google Play Requirements

  • No personal data collection
  • No sensitive permissions
  • No tracking SDKs
  • Payments (if any) handled by Google Play Billing

7. Children’s Privacy

ViewPhoto.TV does not collect personal data and can be safely used by children. However, children should use the service under adult supervision when sharing photos.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements to the service or changes in legal requirements. The latest version will always be available at:

https://viewphoto.tv/privacy

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

support@viewphoto.tv
