ViewPhoto.TV — Show Selected Photos on Any TV via QR Code

ViewPhoto.TV is a browser-based tool that lets you show selected photos on any Smart TV. You open the website on your TV, scan a QR code with your phone, and choose photos from your device, Google Photos, or iCloud Photos. Only the photos you select appear on the TV.

No apps. No accounts. No screen mirroring. Total privacy.

Key Features

  • Works on any Smart TV with a browser
  • Works on iPhone and Android
  • Upload photos from device storage, Google Photos, or iCloud Photos
  • Only selected photos appear on the TV
  • No apps or accounts required
  • No screen mirroring and no notifications on TV
  • Full-screen photo viewer on TV
  • Temporary Cloudflare storage with automatic deletion
  • Multiple users can upload photos via QR code

How ViewPhoto.TV Works

  1. Open viewphoto.tv in your TV’s browser.
  2. A temporary session with a unique QR code appears.
  3. Scan the QR code with your phone.
  4. Select photos from your device, Google Photos, or iCloud Photos.
  5. Only the selected photos appear on the TV in a clean gallery.
  6. You can open any photo in full-screen mode.

Privacy and Security

ViewPhoto.TV does not use screen mirroring. Your notifications, messages, and full photo library never appear on the TV. Only the photos you explicitly select are uploaded.

Photos are stored temporarily on Cloudflare and deleted automatically after the session. No permanent storage, no tracking, and no analytics.

Use Cases

  • Show travel photos on a big screen
  • Share wedding or event photos with guests
  • Display photos during family gatherings
  • Use as a simple TV photo viewer without apps
  • Allow multiple people to upload photos to the same TV

Comparison with Screen Mirroring

Screen mirroring shows your entire phone screen, including notifications, messages, and your full photo library. ViewPhoto.TV shows only the photos you select.

  • AirPlay: entire screen visible, notifications visible
  • Miracast: entire screen visible, notifications visible
  • HDMI: entire screen visible
  • ViewPhoto.TV: only selected photos are shown

Privacy & Security

Your photos are yours. Your gallery stays private. Your data is never stored permanently.

ViewPhoto.TV is designed with privacy at its core - the same philosophy Apple uses for its own products.

What stays private

  • Your entire photo library - it is never shown on TV
  • Your notifications, messages, apps
  • Your browsing, your activity, your identity

How we protect your photos

  • Photos are stored temporarily on Cloudflare during the session
  • Default session lifetime: 180 minutes
  • Automatic deletion when the session ends
  • No permanent storage
  • No tracking, no analytics, no ads
  • When selecting photos from Google Photos or iCloud Photos, the browser’s native photo picker ensures that only the items you explicitly choose are shared with the session - nothing else.

Cloudflare acts only as a secure transport layer. All communication is encrypted via HTTPS.
